## 🔧 Troubleshooting Quick Answers

**Q: How do I change my email everywhere?**
A: Update in Hero.jsx, Contact.jsx, and Footer.jsx. See CUSTOMIZATION_GUIDE.md.

**Q: Can I add more sections?**
A: Yes! Create a component in src/components/ and add to App.jsx.

**Q: How do colors work?**
A: Edit --primary-color in src/App.css. Includes 4 pre-made color schemes.

**Q: How do I make it live online?**
